Tote Bag

Typography

Course

Typography

Completed

Spring 2024

Instructor

Andrew Shuz

Prompt: Pick a motto to design and produce an actual wearable merchandise item using only type. Your motto should be a short sentence or phrase and should express a thought that you would like to tell yourself or someone else every morning.
Response: I designed a tote bag with the verse, "Consider how the wildflowers grow." It’s a wearable reminder to slow down, let go of worry and embrace peace. We are more valuable than the things we often worry about.
Skills Learned: Using typography, hierarchy and contrast to help readers navigate a text Typographic form as a tool for expression and communication. Using Adobe Photoshop to create mockups for professional presentation.
