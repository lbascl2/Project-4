Firm Foundations

Photography

Course

Beginner Photography

Completed

Fall 2024

Instructor

Jeremiah Ariaz

Prompt: Open ended. Encouraged to consider unique groups or settings we have access to.
Response: I chose to capture the hurricane damage St. Eloi Church Catholic church sustained after Hurricane Ida and the nuns who live there. Considering the religious context of my subject I utilized juxtaposition, and light to create a sense of peace and stillness that transcends the physical damage around them. I based the body of work on the following verse: “Everyone who listens to these words of mine and acts on them will be like a wise man who built his house on rock. The rain fell, the floods came, and the winds blew and buffeted the houses. But it did not collapse; it had been set solidly on rock. And everyone who listens to these words of mine but does not act on them will be like a fool who built his house on sand. The rain fell, the floods came, and the winds blew and buffeted the house. And it collapsed and was completely ruined”
Skills Learned: Expanded critical thinking skills to create meaningful photographs that tell a story How to edit photos in Adobe Lightroom How to print using Adobe Bridge and Adobe Photoshop to produce high quality physical copies
